# Measure Your Progress

You can't improve what you don't measure. This page ties the whole framework together.

## Know Your Position

At any point, you sit somewhere on the industry curve:

- **Newbie** — Still learning the basics, building functional skills
- **Worker Bee** — Skilled and earning, but stuck in the 50-150K range
- **Emerging KPI** — Executing the 5 Ps, gaining visibility and traction
- **Key Person of Influence** — Connected, known, trusted, earning exponentially more

Be honest about where you are. That determines what to focus on.

Your lowest score is your bottleneck. That's where to focus next.

## Key Checkpoints

- **Core message defined?** Can you pitch yourself in 30 seconds?
- **Infrastructure set up?** Platforms, brand, content base, product map?
- **Publishing consistently?** What's your weekly output?
- **Products built?** How many of the 4 tiers exist?
- **SALT in place?** Social, Awards, Live events, Third-party?
- **Google test passed?** What does a search of your name show?
- **Partnerships active?** Any brand, product, or distribution deals?

## The Rhythm

Review your scorecard monthly. Update your position quarterly. The goal isn't perfection across all 5 Ps at once — it's consistent forward movement on whichever P needs the most attention right now.
