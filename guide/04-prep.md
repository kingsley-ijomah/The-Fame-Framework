# Prep: Build Your Foundation

You can't execute the 5 Ps if you have nowhere to execute them. This phase is about setting up the infrastructure.

## 1. Choose Your Platforms

You need at least 4 social media platforms where you'll show up regularly. Consider:

- Where does your target audience spend time?
- Which formats suit your strengths (video, writing, audio)?
- Where are the KPIs in your industry already active?

Don't overthink it. Pick four, commit, and adjust later.

## 2. Set Up Your Brand Presence

For each platform:

- Profile photo — consistent across all platforms
- Bio — built from your social pitch (name/same/fame/aim/game)
- Visual identity — colours, fonts, style (keep it simple and consistent)
- Links — point back to your content home base

## 3. Create Your Content Home Base

You need one place you own where all roads lead:

- A website or blog
- A newsletter or email list
- A place where your published work lives long-term

Social platforms are rented land. Your home base is owned land.

## 4. Map Your Product Ecosystem

Before building anything, map out what your 4-tier product ecosystem will look like:

- **Gift** (free) — What will you give away?
- **Product for Prospects** (low cost) — What's the easy first step?
- **Core Offering** (flagship) — What's the main thing you deliver?
- **Product for Clients** (ongoing) — What keeps people with you long-term?

You don't need to build them all now. But you need to know where you're heading.
