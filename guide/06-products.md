# Products

You cannot be a key person of influence while selling your time for money. You have to sell products. Income follows assets, and the most valuable asset today is a product.

## The Product Ecosystem

You need four types of products. Not one — four. Products and services don't make money. Product and service *ecosystems* make money.

### 1. Gift (Free)
Something you give away that people think is great value. Could be a free guide, template, tool, or resource.

**Purpose:** Creates attention.

### 2. Product for Prospects (Low Cost)
A cheap, easy product that doesn't require a huge commitment. It's a powerful first step for anyone who wants to work with you.

**Purpose:** Creates trust.

### 3. Core Offering (Flagship)
The main thing you do. It's what you're known for. Transformational, impressive, and delivers real results.

**Purpose:** Creates transformation.

### 4. Product for Clients (Ongoing)
A subscription, membership, or ongoing service that takes people into the future with you.

**Purpose:** Creates long-term relationships.

## The Mindset Shift

You're not just a service provider — you're a product creator and innovator. Think about how to engineer a better offer than what currently exists in your industry.

Creating a new, hot product is one of the best assets you'll ever have. Better than a house. Better than stocks in someone else's company.

## Building the Ecosystem

- Start with your core offering — that's where transformation happens
- Then create a gift to drive attention to it
- Add a prospect product as a low-risk entry point
- Finally, build the ongoing product to retain clients
