# Key Person of Influence — A Practical Framework

A step-by-step guide to becoming the key person of influence in your industry, based on Daniel Priestley's framework.

## Who This Is For

You're skilled at what you do. You're a solid "worker bee" in your industry. But you've noticed that the people at the top — the most visible, most connected, most in-demand — earn exponentially more. This guide helps you bridge that gap.

## How To Use This Guide

Work through the guide pages in order. Each one is a short read (~2-3 minutes) and builds on the previous.

### Guide

1. [The Industry Shape](guide/01-industry-shape.md) — Why a few people earn exponentially more
2. [Your Core Message](guide/02-core-message.md) — Define who you are and what you stand for
3. [Shadow a KPI](guide/03-shadow-a-kpi.md) — Study someone who's already where you want to be
4. [Prep: Build Your Foundation](guide/04-prep.md) — Set up the infrastructure to execute
5. [Pitching](guide/05-pitching.md) — Enroll people into your ideas
6. [Publishing](guide/06-publishing.md) — Put your ideas into the world
7. [Products](guide/07-products.md) — Stop selling time, start selling value
8. [Profile](guide/08-profile.md) — Be discoverable and credible from a distance
9. [Partnerships](guide/09-partnerships.md) — Multiply through collaboration
10. [Measure Your Progress](guide/10-measure.md) — Know where you stand and what to focus on next
